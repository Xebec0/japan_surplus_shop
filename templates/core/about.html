About Japan Surplus Shop

Welcome to Japan Surplus Shop, your premier destination for high-quality Japanese surplus items. Established in 2020, we specialize in bringing authentic Japanese products to customers worldwide at affordable prices.

Our mission is to provide access to Japan's renowned quality products while promoting sustainability through the reuse and repurposing of surplus items. We carefully select each product in our inventory to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and value.

Our Story

Japan Surplus Shop was born from a passion for Japanese craftsmanship and quality. Our founder, during a trip to Japan, was impressed by the exceptional quality of everyday items and the Japanese concept of "mottainai" (avoiding waste). This inspired the creation of a business that would bring these high-quality surplus items to a global market.

Starting with a small collection of household items, we have grown to offer a wide range of products including electronics, kitchenware, furniture, clothing, and more. Each item in our inventory tells a story of Japanese innovation, design, and quality.

Today, we continue to expand our offerings while maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We're proud to be a bridge between Japanese surplus goods and appreciative customers around the world.

Our Values

Quality

We carefully inspect and select each item to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and functionality.

Customer Satisfaction
